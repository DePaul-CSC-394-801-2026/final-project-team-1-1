Welcome to UpKeep

OUR WHY

This project is a home maintenance reminder system desigined to help users stay organized and proactive about caring for their homes.
This system reminds users when tasks need to be completed, helping prevent missed maintenance and reducing long-term wear and damage. By centralizing all home maintenance needs in one place, this project makes it easier for users to manage responsibilities, save and manage time, and maintain a safe and well-functioning home without having to remember every task.
